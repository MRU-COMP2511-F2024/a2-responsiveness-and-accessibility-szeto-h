FAQ

How is data collected?

All of our data come from gamers who submit their game log volunteerily! The data is then processed by our team and shared on our website, ensuring no personal or identifiable data is leaked.

How do I submit my data?

Thank you for your willingness to share your data with us! To share your data you can simply submit a contact us request through the Contact Us form and we will provide you with instructions.

Is everything here free?

Of course! All information and resources on this website is free, in hopes to build a supportive community.
