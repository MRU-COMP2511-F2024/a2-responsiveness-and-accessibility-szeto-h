About Us

Welcome to RaidMetrics!

With our statistical analysis, you can enjoy your game without spending hours researching!

Our data comes from new and experienced players, with the aim to help other players improve their gameplay and build a friendly, welcoming gaming community.

With RaidMetrics, we can provide gamers an overview of damage output, healing amount, damage taken by other players, so you can have an idea on how to clear the boss quickly. You can also use our statistics to find the best gear and boost needed for each role for the most effective clear.

Mission & Vision
